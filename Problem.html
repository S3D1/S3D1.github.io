☹ Problem ☹


Problem: Everyday waste ends up in landfills. Some materials, such as plastic, take a long time to decompose. Decomposing waste also produces landfill gas (LFG) which is roughly half methane and half carbon dioxide. These greenhouse gasses trap heat. Having greenhouses gasses can be a good thing because they help keep the Earth at a temperature that is suitable for humans; however, the amount of greenhouse gasses are increasing which is causing the Earth to trap too much heat.
